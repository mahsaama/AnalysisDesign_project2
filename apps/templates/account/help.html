If you are a doctor, these tips can help you:
  1. To see all of the requests which are submitted for you and haven’t been accepted, click on All Requests on your sidebar.
  2. To see your active requests (accepted and not finished) which are submitted for you, click on Active Orders on your sidebar. By clicking on Done icon for each request, you can finish them. If your patient paid you in cash, click on Paid icon.
  3. To see your done requests (accepted and finished) which are submitted for you, click on Done Orders on your sidebar.
  4. To accept a request, click on its Accept button on All Requests page.
  5. You can search or filter your orders on Active Orders and Done Orders page, using the search bar and the row at the end of columns.
  6. You can see your wallet charge, or the amount of money you are paid online, in your profile (top left).
  7. To log out of your profile, click on profile icon on top right, then click on Logout.
If you are a patient, these tips can help you:
  1. You can see the history of your orders on your dashboard. Information of each order is written on it.
  2. To submit a request, follow this instruction: By clicking on Submit New Request, first, you can choose the expertise that you want, with its specified price. Then, you can choose a doctor of that expertise. Finally, you write your address and details about your problem and you click on Submit Request. Now your request is submitted and will be considered.
  3. By clicking on Doctors in your sidebar, you will see a list of all of our doctors an their information. By clicking on Profile icon of each doctor, you can see additional information. By clicking on Submit Request icon of each doctor, you can directly submit a request for them. By clicking on Heart icon of each doctor, you can add them to your favorite doctors.
  4. To view your favorite doctors, click on Favorite Doctors on your sidebar. You can remove them from your favorites any time by clicking again on the Heart icon.
  5. To search in doctors, you can use the search field in Doctors. Also, you can filter doctors by different features, using the row at the end of columns.
  6. After your request is accepted by your doctor, you can submit score, comment, or compliment, according to your doctor’s service. Your review of our services is very important to us!
  7. By clicking on Charge Your Wallet, you can charge your wallet in order to pay to your doctor online. After inserting your card info, click on Check Out. Your current wallet charge is visible on your dashboard and also on paying page.
  8. To pay to your doctor in cash, you need to ask them to submit your check out.
  9. To pay to your doctor online, you can click on Pay Online of your request. If your wallet has enough money, it will be paid. Otherwise, you wil be asked to charge your wallet.
  10. If you want to cancel your order, click on Cancel button of that order.
  11. To log out of your profile, click on profile icon on top right, then click on Logout.
