Docicom

DociCom is an online healthcare system which was created during the COVID-19 pandemic in order to help deliver medical services at the comfort of your home and eliminate the need for visiting crowded and high-risk hospitals and clinics. The current version of DociCom is still under development and we appreciate any feedback for improving our services. Thank you very much for supporting DociCom and helping us make medical service available at minimum cost to everyone.
